Final Job Description: Tech Lead - UI Micro-Frontend Development

Position Overview:
We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Tech Lead - UI Micro-Frontend Development to take charge of the design and development of enterprise web applications. As a pivotal member of our team, you will leverage your expertise in React JS and Micro Frontend architecture to architect scalable, modular, and maintainable UI solutions. This role will play a crucial part in upholding the highest standards of code quality, performance, and user experience.

Work Location: WFH or Bangalore / Hyderabad
Total Experience: 7 to 15 Years

Key Responsibilities:

Architectural Design:

Lead the architectural design of robust, modular, and reusable UI components using React JS.
Implement Micro Frontend architecture to enhance flexibility and scalability within the frontend ecosystem.

Continuous Improvement:

Drive continuous improvement initiatives in UI development processes and methodologies.
Champion the adoption of best practices to ensure efficiency and effectiveness in the development lifecycle.

Collaboration:

Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand requirements and align UI development with overall project objectives.
Work closely with UX/UI designers to translate design concepts into seamless and visually appealing user interfaces.

Mandatory Skills:

Demonstrate a strong understanding of designing and implementing Micro Frontend architecture or microservices concepts in UI development.
Showcase expertise in UI development with a focus on React JS.
Familiarity with RESTful APIs and integration with backend services.
Experience with state management libraries such as Redux or Mobx.
Proficiency in modern frontend technologies, including JavaScript (ES6+), HTML5, and CSS3.
Possess a solid understanding of UI Design architecture.
Exhibit strong problem-solving skills and the ability to troubleshoot complex issues.

Additional Skills:

Familiarity with backend technologies, including Spring Boot and Spring MVC.
Understanding of backend/frontend integration and communication protocols.
Knowledge of secure coding practices and the ability to implement them.

Preferred Qualifications:

Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
Certifications in React JS or Micro Frontend development.
Experience with AWS services and infrastructure.
Proficient in CI/CD pipelines and related tools.
Familiarity with containerization and orchestration tools such as Docker and Kubernetes.
Familiarity with server-side rendering (SSR) and progressive web application (PWA) concepts.
Contribution to open-source projects related to UI development.
